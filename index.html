Project 02

COMMON FLU: THE TEST & SIMULATION OF INFLUENZA

This model is designed to provide an accessible and user-friendly way for individuals to determine whether they might be infected with influenza. The test is tailored for casual users and does not require formal health examinations or medical tests. instead, it relies solely on a series of simple questions the user can answer based on their current symptoms and experiences. while the model does not guarantee medical accuracy, it offers predictions by calculating the likelihood of infection as a percentage. Based on the user’s answers, the model will output different risk levels, (i.e. low risk = 0 - 39%, moderate risk = 40-79%, and high risk = 80% and up).
